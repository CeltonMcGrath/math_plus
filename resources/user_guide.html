Welcome to the registration system for the Math+ programs offered by the Department of Mathematics at the University of Toronto.

The purpose of this site is to make it simple and easy for you to sign up for programs offered.

How to Navigate the Site

The Account tab above has information on your account, such as:

  • Email Address and Preference
  • Parent/Guardian Information
  • Student Information and Programs
  • Password Changes

How to Sign Up For Programs

To sign up for a program you must first create a guardian account and then create a student account. Once the student has been added you can click the Add Programs button at the bottom of the student account to be taken to the programs.

Please note that you should only sign up for programs that are at an appropriate grade level for your student. If you want to sign up for a program at a different level please contact our office first as refunds will not be provided for programs at an inappropriate level.

The Cart tab has programs you have selected. You can also delete programs not yet paid for.

If you have applied for and been successfully selected for a bursary you can add the code here.

Once you have selected a program and added it to your cart you will click Proceed to Payment to be taken to our Moneris payment page. You are not registered for a program until you have paid and received an email confirmation from Moneris.

What If I Have Questions

The FAQ tab has a number of Frequently Asked Questions regarding programs, registrations, bursaries and refunds. If you are unsure of a policy please check there. If you can't find an answer to your question there please contact us at outreach@math.toronto.edu

We look forward to seeing you at our programs!
